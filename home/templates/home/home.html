What is Spoti Stats?

No more having to wait to the end of the year for your Spotify Unwrapped.
This web app is a fun way to check your own listening trends on your own terms.
It's built using the Spotify API to scrape user listening data.

Currently, Spoti Stats is able to grab your top tracks and top artists within three different time intervals (short term, medium term, and long term listening history).
